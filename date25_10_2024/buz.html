And we're back.. India have a quick huddle before taking the field. Australia are without Warner, Marsh and Stoinis. Also, Cameron Green is going to open with Finch and he's doing it for the first time in his career. How will his mindset be after that last over? Bhuvneshwar Kumar is at the top of his mark and he loves bowling to Finch. Karthik is up to the stumps. There's a slip in place. Off we go..

KL Rahul: Looks a good pitch. It took some time to get used to the bounce of the pitch. I have played here in the IPL and I knew the shots that work here. I used my experience. I always work on hitting on both sides of the ground and get into good positions. Fortunately the ball was in the areas where I could hit it for sixes. Glad they came off the middle of the bat.

20:45 Local Time, 15:15 GMT: Having been put into bat, India have done exceedingly well to cross the 200-mark and if you add those two wickets they lost in the powerplay then it makes it an even better finish. KL Rahul lost Rohit and Kohli inside the first six overs but that didn't stop from showing intent, he raced to a 32-ball half century and stitched a 68-run stand with SKY. The pair had set the platform for Hardik to go ballistic and he did just that. He hammered a 25-ball fifty and even managed to record his career-best T20I score. This looks a belter of a surface and with dew expected it is going to be a cracking chase. We'll be back in a jiffy..
